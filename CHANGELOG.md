## [3.0.0] - 2016-11-14
### Changed

- DBus service, interface, and path are now net.retroj.mowedline,
  net.retroj.mowedline, and /net/retroj/mowedline.
- Use dbus2 egg instead of dbus egg.
  
### Added

- Support org.freedesktop.DBus.Introspectable.
